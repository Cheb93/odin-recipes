Classic Milanesa




Ingredients

  • Chicken Steak
  • Egg
  • Ground Bread
  • Oil
  • Salt


Cooking steps

  1. Beat the egg.
  2. Deep the chicken steak in the egg.
  3. Expand the chicken steak over the grounded bread.
  4. Let the bread crumbs cover the chicken steak.
  5. Turn it around and cover the other face.
  6. Add some salt to the breaded chicken steak.
  7. Heat a pan with oil.
  8. Cook one side of the breaded chicken steak for about 3 minutes.
  9. Cook the other side.
  10. Serve.

    note: you can dry some of the oil from the breaded chicken steak with absorbent paper
